Customer Segmentation — SQL & Power BI

Problem: Prioritize marketing spend by identifying highest lifetime value customers.

Dataset: Orders and customer profile tables.

Tools: SQL (joins, aggregations), Power BI (dashboarding).

Approach

Calculated customer-level metrics (recency, frequency, monetary), performed RFM-style segmentation in SQL, and built a Power BI dashboard to explore segments.

Key Results

  • Top 10% of customers contributed ~45% of revenue.
  • Recommended targeted retention campaigns for high-LTV segment, estimated +8% retention uplift.
