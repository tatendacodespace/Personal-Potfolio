Sales Analysis — Excel

Problem: Identify drivers of revenue and seasonal patterns for inventory planning.

Dataset: Transactional sales CSV (date, SKU, region, revenue).

Tools: Excel — pivot tables, formulas, charts.

Approach

Cleaned the dataset, normalized product categories, used pivot tables to aggregate sales by time and category, and prepared charts to reveal trends.

Key Results

  • Top 3 categories accounted for ~62% of revenue.
  • Clear seasonal uplift in Q4; recommended increasing inventory for top SKUs.
  • Identified low-performing SKUs for potential delisting.
